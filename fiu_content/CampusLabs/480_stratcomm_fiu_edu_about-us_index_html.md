![](https://stratcomm.fiu.edu/_assets/images/about-work-strategically.jpg)
## Working strategically
The Division of Strategic Communications, Government and External Affairs is charged with increasing public awareness of FIU through effective marketing and communication strategies that enhance the university’s image and present a consistent, unified message. Each of our departments is integral to moving our university forward through effective communication, message development and the cultivation of affinity and pride in internal and external audiences.
