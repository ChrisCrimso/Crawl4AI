# Contact
 _FIU News_ is maintained and updated daily by the Office of News & Communications. Many of our writers are expert communicators placed in your colleges and units.
We value your suggestions and feedback. Got an idea for a story, video, or photo you would like to see on FIU News? Email news@fiu.edu. We’ll be in touch soon.
