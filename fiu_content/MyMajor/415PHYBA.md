Program Description
The Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in Physics prepares students interested in physics and planning to enter professional schools in business, education, journalism, law, and medicine, and for students desiring a strong background in physical science but with career objectives in other areas. It is available with concentrations in biophysics, business, and entrepreneurship. The B.A. in Physics with a Major in Physics Education prepares those who wish to teach grades 6-12 and allows the students to complete all the requirements to be fully certified by the State of Florida.
