## FIU Athletics Hall of Fame Nomination Form
  * [Print](https://fiusports.com/sb_output.aspx?form=49)


![FIU Athletics Hall of Fame Logo](https://fiusports.com/images/2025/2/5/FIU_Athletics_HOF_Primary_Logo.png)
The FIU Athletics Hall of Fame was created to recognize and honor FIU Athletics Student-Athletes, Coaches, Teams, Administrators and Sports Contributors for their achievements and/or contributions to the FIU Athletic program. The deadline to submit nominees for potential inclusion in the Class of 2025 is March 15, 2025. Moving forward, the cutoff each year will be February 15. Any submissions after that date will be considered for induction the following year. Prior to submitting a nomination, please review the **[FIU Athletics Hall of Fame Constitution by clicking here](https://fiusports.com/documents/2025/2/5/FIU_Athletics_Hall_of_Fame_Constitution.pdf)**. This document outlines criteria required to be met before a nominee may be considered for selection into the FIU Athletics Hall of Fame.
FIU Athletics Hall of Fame Nomination Form
  1. Hidden name field
  2. Submitter's Contact Information
  3. Name*
  4. Email Address*
  5. Phone Number*
  6. Mailing Address
  7. Nominee
  8. Name Of Individual Or Team Being Nominated*
  9. Nominee's Contact Information (If Known)
  10. Email Address
  11. Phone Number
  12. Mailing Address
  13. Background Information On Nominee
  14. Award Category* Select...Student-Athlete (Criteria: 10 Years)Team (Criteria: 5 Years)Coach (Criteria: 5 Years)Administrator (Criteria: 5 Years)Sports Contributor (Criteria: 5 Years)
  15. Year Graduated / Retired / Departed FIU OR Year Of Accomplishment*
  16. Years Attended / Served FIU Athletics*
  17. Sports Participated In / Coached / Area Of Focus*
  18. FIU Athletics Honors & Recognitions*
  19. Records Established / Held At FIU*
  20. Professional Activity Post-FIU
  21. Additional Supporting Information
  22. Submit Form
