## A Primer on the Mathematics of Environmental Financial Engineering
[ Mathematics and Statistical Sciences/CASE ](https://catalog.fiu.edu/departments/MATHSTAT/overview) Graduate CASE - Col of Arts,Sciences&Education
Overview  Requirement Information 
### Subject code
MAP
### Catalog Number
5620
### Course Title
Math Primer for Env Fin
### Career
Graduate
### Course Description
This course is an introductory course that presents the mathematical tools necessary for financial instrument, pricing and hedging design. Prerequisite: Working knowledge of Calculus and Basic Statistics and Probability.
