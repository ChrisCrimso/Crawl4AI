## 19th Century Latin American Literature
[ Modern Languages/SIPA ](https://catalog.fiu.edu/departments/MODLANG-2/overview) Graduate SIPA - Green Sch of Intl & Public Aff
Overview  Requirement Information 
### Subject code
SPW
### Catalog Number
5475
### Course Title
19Th C Lat Amer Lit
### Career
Graduate
### Course Description
A study of the main literary works of Spanish speaking 19th Century Latin America: Romanticism, Realism, Naturalism and Modernism. Prerequisites: Upper level and graduate standing.
Credits
### Minimum Credits
3
### Maximum Credits
3
