# Master of Science in Information Systems
The Master of Science in Information Systems (MSIS) is a **STEM-designated program** that equips you with advanced technical skills and practical expertise in managing information systems. With this designation, international students who hold an F-1 visa can stay in the U.S. for up to three years after graduation through Optional Practical Training (OPT) and the STEM OPT extension. The program prepares you to lead digital transformation efforts and excel in high-level management roles within the field. Our graduates are making an impact as CIOs, CISOs, IT directors, information security administrators, business analysts, IT project managers, and cybersecurity systems analysts.
[Learn more about career paths and salaries with a master’s in information systems](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/graduate/insights/posts/should-you-pursue-a-master-of-science-in-information-systems.html)
**Choose your Specialization Track**
  * Business Analytics
  * Business Cybersecurity
  * Artificial Intelligence
  * Digital Transformation


Learn more about the specialization [tracks and curriculum](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/graduate/information-systems/curriculum/index.html).
