# Master of Accounting
FIU’s Master of Accounting (MACC) program is open to those who majored in accounting or business in college and offers specializations in **taxation** and **assurance**.
With convenient hybrid, or [fully online classes](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/graduate/accounting-online/index.html), professionals easily balance career development among their other responsibilities. The MACC program satisfies the 30 credit hours required beyond a bachelor’s degree for Florida CPA licensure. Additionally, students gain access to the Becker CPA Course, providing them with the tools to excel on the CPA exam.
As a cohort-based, lock-step program, you’ll build strong relationships with a diverse group of students both during and long after your program.
You will have full access to Business Career Services resources, helping you gain the knowledge and professional skills to be even more competitive in today's challenging job market.
[Path for Other Business Majors](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/graduate/accounting/path-for-other-business-majors/index.html) pre-requisite courses are offered fully online each summer & fall.

## Program at a Glance MACC for Accounting Majors
Program Duration 10 months
Two Specialization Majors Taxation and Assurance
Intakes Program starts each fall
Schedule Hybrid (a blend of on-campus and online classes).In-person sessions: Tuesday & Thursday evenings at the MMC campus.
Tuition in-person Florida residents: $36,000 approximately 
Non-residents: $39,000 approximately
