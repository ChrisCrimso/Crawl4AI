# The Professional MBA
Chart your own career path, even as you balance demands of work, family, and school. The Professional MBA is designed with the working professional in mind and offers three scheduling options:
**[Professional MBA Weekend](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/graduate/professional-mba-weekend/index.html)** Earn your MBA in just 16 months! Saturday classes are held at our FIU at I-75 location in Miramar with online flexibility.
**[Professional MBA Downtown](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/graduate/professional-mba-downtown/index.html)** Earn your MBA in just 16 months! Evening classes are held twice a week at our FIU Downtown on Brickell location.
**[Professional MBA Flex](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/graduate/professional-mba-flex/index.html)** Earn your MBA in 20 months! Evening classes are held once a week at our FIU Downtown on Brickell location.
