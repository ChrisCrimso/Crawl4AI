## Need help?
### How to register for Students and Alumni
  1. Click on [FIU Business - Handshake](https://fiu.joinhandshake.com/register) to claim your account.
  2. On the next screen, click on 'Student/Alumni'
  3. Then fill out the required fields **using your FIU email address**.


### How to register as an Employer
  1. Access [FIU Business - Handshake](https://fiu.joinhandshake.com/register)
  2. Set up an employer account
  3. Our FIU Career Team will review and approve your account, ensuring you meet basic requirements such as having a company email and physical office space
  4. Post a job, register for career fairs, and more!


If you can't log in to Handshake to set up your account, contact our office at 305-348-7396 or via email at cms@fiu.edu.
