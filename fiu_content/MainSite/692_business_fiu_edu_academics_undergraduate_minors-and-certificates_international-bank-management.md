# Certificate in International Bank Management
The Certificate in International Bank Management (CIBM) offers specialized training to equip students for success in global banking. The CIBM program includes four undergraduate finance courses on financial risk management, international financial management, international banking and your choice of an elective in either commercial bank management or credit analysis and loan evaluation.
 _Also available to fully online students._

  * [Who should enroll?](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/#panel-N10548-1)
Any business student who wants to complement their major field of study with additional insights into international banking.
  * [Courses](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/#panel-N10548-2)
A minimum GPA of 2.75 and admissibility to the College of Business is required.
**Prerequisites**
[FIN 3403](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Financial Management)[ACG 2021](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Accounting for Decisions)[ECO 2013](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Principles of Macro Economics)[FIN 3414](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Intermediate Finance)[FIN 4502](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Securities Analysis)
**CIBM Certificate Courses**
[FIN 4486](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) Financial Risk Management-Financial Engineering[FIN 4604](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) International Financial Management[FIN 4634](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) International Banking[FIN 4324](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) Commercial Bank Management, or [FIN 4345](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) Credit Analysis and Loan Evaluation (only offered online), or [FIN 4663](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) Global Private Banking (only offered on-campus, not online)
  * [Admission Requirements](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/#panel-N10548-3)
To apply for admission to this program, please email Ira Turner for more information.
  * [Certificate in Banking and Certificate International Bank Management (Dual Option)](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/#panel-N10548-4)
This is for students wishing to take both certificates at the same time. Note: You can’t use the same course to satisfy both program requirements. If you are a fully online student you will not be able to satisfy the DUAL requirements because not all courses are offered online.
### Courses
A minimum GPA of 2.75 and admissibility to the College of Business is required.
**Prerequisites**
[FIN 3403](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Financial Management)[ACG 2021](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Accounting for Decisions)[ECO 2013](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Principles of Macro Economics)[FIN 3414](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Intermediate Finance)[FIN 4502](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) (Securities Analysis)
**DUAL Certificate Courses (7) No substitutions nor exceptions**
[FIN 4486](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) Financial Risk Management-Financial Engineering and[FIN 4303](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) Financial Markets and Institutions and[FIN 4324](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) Commercial Bank Management and[FIN 4604](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) International Financial Management and[FIN 4634](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) International Banking and[FIN 4345](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) Credit Analysis and Loan Evaluation (only offered online) and[FIN 4663](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/undergraduate/minors-and-certificates/international-bank-management/) Global Private Banking (only offered on-campus, not online)
