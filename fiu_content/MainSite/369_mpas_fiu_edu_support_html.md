# Social Justice & Inclusion
In compliance with Section 1004.06 of Florida Statutes, the Office of Social Justice & Inclusion (OSJI) has been closed. FIU remains committed to supporting student success and fostering a culture of belonging. As such, educational training and cultural programming related to the Pride Center, Women’s Center, and Men's Engagement Center permitted by the Florida Statutes have been transitioned to the [Centers for Student Engagement](https://dasa.fiu.edu/all-departments/centers-for-student-engagement/index.html) (CSE).
