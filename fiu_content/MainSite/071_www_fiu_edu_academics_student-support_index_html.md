## We're Here to Help
Tough classes, new stresses and lots of uncertainty. College life brings lots of great experiences, but it also presents lots of new challenges. We've all been there and we know everyone needs a helping hand. Explore our support services and get to know how we can help:
  * [Advising](https://dasa.fiu.edu/all-departments/advising/)
  * [Career Services](https://career.fiu.edu/)
  * [College Life Coaching](https://dasa.fiu.edu/all-departments/college-life-coaching/)
  * [Counseling & Psychological Services](http://studentaffairs.fiu.edu/health-and-fitness/counseling-and-psychological-services/)
