# Online Master of Science in Marketing
Business doesn't stand still. It's an ever-changing landscape, with the rapid evolution of digital and mobile technologies and increasingly sophisticated consumers. The Online Master of Science in Marketing program at FIU Business has a specialized focus on three key strategic areas that are essential in today’s business environment: digital marketing, brand development and marketing analytics.
Earn your degree fully online, and emerge with the technical knowledge, deep analytical skills and versatility required to be a marketing leader. Graduates are ready to launch careers as marketing managers, account executives, digital media specialists, brand managers, marketing analysts and social media directors.
According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, market analysts and marketing specialists are projected to be among the fastest growing occupations, with a projected 31.6% growth rate over the next 10 years. The entry-level salary for a market analyst is $63,120 and the median salary for marketing managers is $136,850.
The Master of Science in Marketing is also available in a [hybrid format](https://business.fiu.edu/academics/graduate/marketing/index.html).
