# PhD in Psychology: Developmental Science
Our Doctor of Philosophy in Psychology with a major in Developmental Science provides students with the skills to describe and explain change over time in humans and other organisms. Students gain knowledge of cutting-edge developmental methods and theories while developing expertise in their speciality topic area through a one-on-one mentor model.
