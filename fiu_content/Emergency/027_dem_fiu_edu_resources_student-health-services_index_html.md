# FIU Student Health Clinics
The Student Health Clinics provide affordable and accessible student-focused medical care and promote healthy lifestyles through education, mentorship, and research activities thus facilitating the academic success of our students. We proactively assess our diverse population and work with university and community partners to address the changing needs of our students, in a holistic, innovative, and supportive environment where optimal health can be realized. Visit us on the Modesto A. Maidique Campus (MMC) or the Biscayne Bay Campus (BBC).
## Modesto A. Maidique Campus
Student Health Center (SHC)11200 SW 8th StreetMiami, FL 33199Monday - Friday: 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. (ET)
## Biscayne Bay Campus
Health Care Center (HCC)3000 NE 151 StreetNorth Miami, FL 33181Monday - Friday:8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.(ET)
## In case of emergency
**Clinic hours currently are Monday - Friday: 8 a.m. - 5 p.m.** For clinical, non-urgent issues outside of business hours, please call our after-hours line 305-348-2401, option 2. If you are _faculty or staff_ , please reach out to FIU Health at 305-348-3267.
If you experience a medical emergency, call the police as follows:
**On-campus FIU emergencies**
**MMC: 305-348-5911 or BBC: 305-919-5911**
