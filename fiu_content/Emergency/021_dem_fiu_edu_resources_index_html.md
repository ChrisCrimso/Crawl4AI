# Resources
This section contains resources such as training, planning tools, and external sources related to emergency preparedness.
[**FIU Ready**](https://dem.fiu.edu/resources/fiu-ready/index.html)
Is an online continuity of operation planning tool that assists FIU units in creating continuity of operation plans.
**[Counseling and Psychological Services](https://dem.fiu.edu/resources/counseling-and-psychological-services/index.html)**
FIU's Counseling & Psychological Services (CAPS) provides mental health services to students that will facilitate and enhance their personal learning, emotional well-being, and academic skills development. 
**[Student Health Clinics](https://dem.fiu.edu/resources/student-health-services/index.html "Student Health Services")**
The Student Health Clinics provide affordable and accessible student-focused medical care and promote healthy lifestyles through education, mentorship, and research activities thus facilitating the academic success of our students. 
[**Safety Equipment**](https://dem.fiu.edu/resources/safety-equipment/index.html)
FIU takes safety seriously and has implemented equipment for calling for help, assisting individuals with impaired mobility to evacuate and equipment to prevent cardiac arrests. 
The latest hurricane preparedness facts and advice from Miami Dade County's Office of Emergency Management.
[**Environmental Health and Safety**](https://ehs.fiu.edu/training/index.html)
FIU's Department of Environmental Health and Safety (EHS) offers courses covering a wide range of topics, from lab safety and hazardous materials handling to general campus safety. These trainings provide essential knowledge to help ensure a safe and compliant campus environment.
[**FIU Police Department**](https://police.fiu.edu/services/)
The FIU Police Department provides several services to the FIU community, including active shooter awareness and preparation, self-defense awareness and familiarization programs, and more.
