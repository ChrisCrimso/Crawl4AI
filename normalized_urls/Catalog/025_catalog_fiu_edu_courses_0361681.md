## 3D Digital Sculpture and Experimental Virtual Environments
[ Art & Art History/CARTA ](https://catalog.fiu.edu/departments/ARTAHST-2/overview) Graduate CARTA - Coll of Comm, Arch + The Arts
Overview  Requirement Information 
### Subject code
ART
### Catalog Number
5668
### Course Title
3D Digital Sculpture
### Career
Graduate
### Course Description
An advanced study of 3D digital sculpture and new media with emphasis on fundamental strategies for making experiments in digital modeling, 3D printing and assemblage of mixed media. May be repeated.
Credits
### Minimum Credits
3
### Maximum Credits
3
### Repeat for Credit
Yes
