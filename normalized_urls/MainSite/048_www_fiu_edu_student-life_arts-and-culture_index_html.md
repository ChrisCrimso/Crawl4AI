# Arts & Culture Find Your Creative _Spark_

## Experience a World of Culture Right on Campus
Reflecting Miami's dynamic culture, FIU is home to performances and appearances by world-renowned artists and the world's foremost thinkers.
## Theater
Experience Shakespeare or, perhaps, something more avant-garde. Our award-winning productions bring stories to life.
[See what's playing now](http://carta.fiu.edu/theatre/productions-news/productions/now-playing/)
![](https://www.fiu.edu/_assets/images/student-life/theater2.jpg)
### Renowned Facilities
[Herbert and Nicole Wertheim Performing Arts Center](http://carta.fiu.edu/wpac/) - WPAC features an intimate, 300-seat main stage, instrumental hall, recital hall, black box studio, 25 practice rooms and more. It's considered one of Miami's premier collegiate concert and performance venues.
[Mary Ann Wolfe Theatre](https://dasa.fiu.edu/all-departments/wolfe-university-center/host-an-event/index.html) - The Mary Ann Wolfe Theater is a multi-purpose facility that seats approximately 300 and plays host to a variety of different concerts, plays, and other events.
[Royal@FIU World Stage Collaborative](https://carta.fiu.edu/theatre/discover/royalfiu-world-stage/) - A 130,000-square-foot, state-of-the-art rehearsal and production studio, Royal@FIU features a 300-seat theater.
## Music
From classical to electronic, FIU performances have the full spectrum of sound covered.
[FIU Bayfest: The Music Festival](https://dasa.fiu.edu/all-departments/student-programming-council/) - With Biscayne Bay in the background, Bayfest features battling DJs, local and international acts, food trucks and more.
[Miami International GuitART Festival ](http://migf.fiu.edu/) - Showcasing world-renowned artists, luthiers and the talents of FIU's School of music, MIGF is a celebration of the guitar.
[New Music Miami ISCM Festival](http://carta.fiu.edu/music/concerts-news/new-music-miami-iscm-festival/) - presents new music for diverse instrumentation throughout Miami, bringing together innovative composers and thinkers along with adventurous performers and audiences.
[FIU Festival of Bands ](http://carta.fiu.edu/music/concerts-news/fiu-festival-of-bands/) - South Florida's best school bands take the stage alongside FIU's own performers.

## Visual Arts
Our collections and exhibitions let you experience the world through the lens of art and history.
![](https://www.fiu.edu/_assets/images/student-life/jewish-museum.jpg)
[Jewish Museum of Florida](https://jmof.fiu.edu/) - The Jewish Museum of Florida-FIU explores the Jewish experience in Florida; how Florida Jews have influenced state, the nation, and the world; and the issues of discrimination against all peoples in Florida history.
![](https://www.fiu.edu/_assets/images/locations/miami-beach-urban-studios.jpg)
[Miami Beach Urban Studios](https://www.fiu.edu/locations/miami-beach-urban-studios/index.html) - Provides an expansive practice and performance space for music and theatre students as well expansive gallery/exhibition spaces.
![](https://www.fiu.edu/_assets/images/student-life/frost-art-museum.jpg)
[Patricia & Phillip Frost Art Museum](https://frost.fiu.edu/exhibitions-events/exhibitions/index.html) - Designed by world-renowned architect Yann Weymouth, the Patricia & Phillip Frost Art Museum at Modesto A. Maidique Campus counts nearly 6,000 objects of art in its permanent collection and hosts a variety of exhibitions.
![](https://www.fiu.edu/_assets/images/student-life/wolfsonian.jpg)
- The Wolfsonian–FIU, located on Washington Avenue in Miami Beach, is a museum, library and research facility. The museum serves as a resource for students to observe, study and research our impressive collection of art and historical pieces.
### FIU Museums Insiders Program
Those currently enrolled in Florida International University undergraduate and graduate programs are invited to stand out as FIU Museums Insiders, an exclusive student community of culture lovers, designers, and thought leaders. Participation is 100% free and unlocks:
  * Free admission for you +2 during your visit
  * Coupon for complimentary swag
  * Alerts on student work opportunities
  * Invites to events and experiences

[Steven & Dorothea Green Lecture Series](https://frost.fiu.edu/learn/lectures/) – Internationally recognized artists, curators, scholars and critics speak on a variety of art topics.
